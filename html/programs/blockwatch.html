Blockwatch

Block Watch is a national program that is based on the principle that neighbors working together are the first and best line of defense against crime.

This page contains information about Block Watch and templates you can use to start your own Neighborhood Block Watch.

Special thanks to the Tulalip Tribes Office of Neighborhoods for providing their Block Watch materials. All the materials are in Word .doc format. Click on link to open or download.
